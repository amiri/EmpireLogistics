List of trade unions in the United States

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

This is a list of trade unions in the United States. Unions exist to represent the interests of workers, who form the membership. Under US labor law, the National Labor Relations Act 1935 is the primary statute which gives US unions rights. The rights of members are governed by the Labor Management Reporting and Disclosure Act 1959.
